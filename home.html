Smashing Gender Stereotypes

#Breakthebias

Just the way it is?

According to the UN, a significant gender gap still persists within science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) disciplines all over the world.

This gender gap is influenced by harmful stereotypes and prejudices which act to block opportunities for women and persons identifying as female.

In conjuction with the Code First Girls (CFG) ethos of creating a more balanced representation of women in tech, this international women’s day we want to fight back against gender stereotyping in STEM.

Join us this international women day to #breakthebias of women in tech.

"Science is not a boy's game, it's not a girl's game.
It's everyone's game.”
